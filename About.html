→ About Me


Hi, I'm Parsi Sricharan, a 17-year-old passionate and curious developer-in-training on a mission to master full-stack development and build cool, meaningful projects from scratch. I'm also into video editing.

Currently on a mission to complete all of Harvard's CS50 courses and master the fundamentals of computer science.

Right now, I'm learning the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js) and Data Structures & Algorithms through Apna College's Sigma 8.0 course. I’m also diving deep into programming with C, C++, Python, and Kotlin — aiming to explore everything from web apps to Android development and even AI/ML in the future.

My journey into coding started with a simple curiosity about how things work. I’ve always loved tinkering with technology, and that’s what drives me to take on new challenges every day.

I’ve completed the first few weeks of Harvard’s CS50x course, which has given me a solid foundation in computer science and problem-solving. Whether it’s optimizing code, customizing a low-spec desktop, or building something small but functional — I enjoy learning by doing.

If I’m not coding, you’ll find me watching anime, leveling up in casual games, or catching up on YouTube tech and News videos.

This site is a space to share what I’m learning, what I’m building, and where I’m headed. Thanks for stopping by!
